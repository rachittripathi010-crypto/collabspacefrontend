This Privacy Policy explains how Tamperline.us ("Tamperline", "we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, stores, and protects personal data when you access or use our brewery and retail management platform, website, and related services (collectively, the "Services").

We are committed to protecting your privacy and complying with applicable data protection laws, including the UK GDPR, EU GDPR, California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), and other global privacy regulations required by major payment gateways.

02

2. Information We Collect

a. Information You Provide

  • Name, email address, phone number
  • Company or business details
  • Account login credentials
  • Billing and subscription details
  • Communications with support
  • Data you upload or manage within the platform

b. Information Collected Automatically

  • IP address and approximate location
  • Device, browser, and operating system details
  • Usage data (features used, timestamps, logs)
  • Cookies and similar tracking technologies

c. Payment Information

Tamperline.us does not store or process card or bank details directly. Payments are securely handled by third-party payment gateways. We may receive limited transaction metadata (such as payment status or subscription ID) for billing and compliance purposes.

03

3. How We Use Your Information

We use personal data to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve the Services
  • Process payments and manage subscriptions
  • Send important service notifications, low-stock alerts, booking confirmations, and updates
  • Detect and prevent fraud or unauthorized account access
  • Respond to your inquiries and provide customer support
  • Personalize your dashboard experience (preferences, templates, saved filters)
  • Comply with legal obligations under applicable law
04

4. Custom Data in the CRM

All operational data you enter into Tamperline.us — including batch logs, inventory records, production recipes, customer booking information, and supplier details — is your data. We treat it as strictly confidential:

  • Your recipes and batch formulas are never shared with other users or third parties
  • Customer booking data is never used for marketing purposes by Tamperline.us
  • Inventory and stock data is never sold or benchmarked in any identifiable form
  • You can export all your data in CSV/JSON format at any time from account settings
  • We access your data only to provide support or as required by applicable law
05

5. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to keep you signed in and improve your experience. You can manage preferences through your browser or our cookie consent tool. We use:

  • Essential Cookies: Required for login sessions and platform security
  • Analytics Cookies: Help us understand which features are used most (e.g., Google Analytics)
  • Preference Cookies: Remember your dashboard settings, unit preferences, and filters

You may disable non-essential cookies at any time through your browser settings. Note that disabling essential cookies may affect platform functionality.

06

6. Data Storage and Hosting

Your data is stored on secure cloud servers provided by trusted hosting providers with enterprise-grade security certifications (SOC 2, ISO 27001). We maintain:

  • AES-256 encryption for all data at rest
  • TLS 1.3 encryption for all data in transit between your browser and our servers
  • Daily automated backups stored redundantly in multiple secure geographic locations
  • 99.9% uptime SLA backed by redundant cloud infrastructure
07

7. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal or business data. We may share limited data only in these circumstances:

  • Service Providers: Trusted providers who help operate Tamperline.us (cloud hosting, email delivery, payment processing) — under strict Data Processing Agreements
  • Legal Requirements: Legal authorities when required by law, court order, or regulatory requirement
  • Business Transfers: A business acquirer in case of a merger or acquisition — with advance notice provided to you
08

8. International Data Transfer

Your data may be transferred to and processed in countries outside your home country, including the United States. When transferring data internationally, we ensure appropriate safeguards are in place, including:

  • Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) approved by the European Commission
  • Adequacy decisions by the European Commission where applicable
  • Binding Corporate Rules or other legally recognized transfer mechanisms

By using the Services, you acknowledge and consent to the transfer of your information as described in this policy.

09

9. Data Security

We apply industry-standard security measures to protect your brewery's data:

  • AES-256 encryption for all data at rest (inventory records, batch logs, bookings)
  • TLS 1.3 encryption for all data in transit between your browser and our servers
  • Regular third-party security audits and penetration testing
  • Role-based access controls so your staff only see what they need to
  • Daily automated backups stored redundantly in multiple locations
  • Incident response procedures and breach notification protocols in compliance with GDPR
10

10. Data Retention

We retain your account and personal data for as long as your Tamperline.us subscription is active. After account deletion, all personal data is securely purged within 90 days. Your exported data files (recipes, batch logs, inventory exports) are yours to keep permanently once downloaded.

Some data may be retained longer if required by applicable law (e.g., financial records for tax compliance purposes, typically 7 years). In such cases, we retain only the minimum data required by law.

11

11. Children's Privacy

Tamperline.us is not intended for individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such data, please contact us immediately and we will take steps to delete it promptly.

12

12. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you have the following rights over your personal data:

  • Access: Request a copy of all personal data we hold about you and your account
  • Rectification: Correct any inaccurate or incomplete information
  • Erasure: Request deletion of your account and all associated personal data
  • Portability: Export your data (inventory, batches, bookings) in a machine-readable format
  • Objection: Object to certain types of processing, such as analytics tracking
  • Restriction: Request that we limit how we process your personal data
ℹ️ To exercise any of these rights, go to Settings → Privacy in your Tamperline.us account, or contact us using the details below.

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with a revised "Last updated" date. Continued use of the Services indicates acceptance of the updated policy. For material changes, we will notify you by email at least 14 days in advance.
