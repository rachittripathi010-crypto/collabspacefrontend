Your brewery's data is your competitive advantage. Tamperline.us (operated by Tamperline.us) takes the privacy of your inventory records, batch recipes, and customer bookings seriously. We do not sell your data — ever. Your recipes and operational data are yours alone.

01

Overview

Tamperline.us, operated by Tamperline.us, acts as the data controller for personal information processed through our platform. We are committed to handling your data transparently and in compliance with applicable privacy laws including GDPR and CCPA. This policy covers all data you and your team enter into Tamperline.us, including inventory logs, batch records, booking details, and account information.

02

Data We Collect

We collect the following categories of information when you use Tamperline.us:

  • Account Data: Your name, business/brewery name, email address, and hashed password during registration
  • Usage Data: Features used, dashboard visits, session durations, and interaction logs to improve the platform
  • Device & Technical Data: IP address, browser type, operating system, and device identifiers
  • Payment Data: Billing details processed securely via our payment provider (Stripe) — we never store raw card numbers
ℹ️ We collect only the minimum personal data necessary to provide and improve Tamperline.us. Your operational data (recipes, stock, bookings) is covered separately below.
03

Your Brewery Data

All operational data you enter into Tamperline.us — including ingredient inventory, batch logs, production recipes, keg and packaging records, customer booking information, and supplier details — is your data. We treat it as strictly confidential and apply the following protections:

  • Your recipes and batch formulas are never shared with other Tamperline.us users or third parties
  • Customer booking data is never used for marketing purposes by Tamperline.us
  • Inventory and stock data is never benchmarked or sold in aggregated form that could identify your business
  • You can export all of your data in CSV/JSON format at any time from your account settings
04

How We Use Your Data

We use your personal information for the following purposes only:

  • Providing, maintaining, and improving the Tamperline.us platform
  • Processing subscription payments and sending transaction receipts
  • Sending important service notifications — low-stock alerts, booking confirmations, and platform updates
  • Detecting and preventing fraud or unauthorized account access
  • Personalizing your dashboard (e.g., remembering preferred units, batch templates)
  • Complying with legal obligations under applicable law
05

Cookies

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to keep you signed in and improve your experience. You can manage preferences through your browser or our cookie consent tool. We use:

  • Essential Cookies: Required for login sessions and platform security
  • Analytics Cookies: Help us understand which features breweries use most (e.g., Google Analytics)
  • Preference Cookies: Remember your dashboard settings, unit preferences, and filters
06

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal or business data. We may share limited data only in these circumstances:

  • Trusted service providers who help operate Tamperline.us (cloud hosting, email delivery, payment processing) — under strict Data Processing Agreements
  • Legal authorities when required by law, court order, or regulatory requirement
  • A business acquirer in case of a merger or acquisition — with advance notice to you
07

Data Security

We apply industry-standard security measures to protect your brewery's data:

  • AES-256 encryption for all data at rest (inventory records, batch logs, bookings)
  • TLS 1.3 encryption for all data in transit between your browser and our servers
  • Regular third-party security audits and penetration testing
  • Role-based access controls so your staff only see what they need to
  • Daily automated backups stored redundantly in multiple locations
  • 99.9% uptime SLA backed by redundant cloud infrastructure
08

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you have the following rights over your personal data:

  • Access: Request a copy of all personal data we hold about you and your account
  • Rectification: Correct any inaccurate or incomplete information
  • Erasure: Request deletion of your account and all associated personal data
  • Portability: Export your brewery data (inventory, batches, bookings) in a machine-readable format
  • Objection: Object to certain types of processing, such as analytics tracking
✅ To exercise any of these rights, go to Settings → Privacy in your Tamperline.us account or contact our Data Protection Officer below.
09

Data Retention

We retain your account and personal data for as long as your Tamperline.us subscription is active. After account deletion, all personal data is securely purged within 90 days. Your exported brewery data files (recipes, batch logs, inventory exports) are yours to keep permanently once downloaded. Some data may be retained longer if required by applicable law.
