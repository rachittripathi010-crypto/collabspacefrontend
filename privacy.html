Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains what personal data CollabSpace collects, why we collect it, and how we protect it. We do not sell your data — ever.

01

Overview

CollabSpace, operated by Tamperline.us, acts as the data controller for personal information processed through our platform. We are committed to handling your data transparently and in compliance with applicable privacy laws including GDPR and CCPA.

02

Data We Collect

We collect the following categories of personal information:

  • Account Data: Name, email address, and password (hashed) during registration
  • Usage Data: Pages visited, features used, session duration, and interaction logs
  • Device & Technical Data: IP address, browser type, operating system, and device identifiers
  • Communication Data: Messages and files shared within your workspace (encrypted at rest)
  • Payment Data: Billing information processed securely via our payment provider (Stripe)
ℹ️ We collect only the minimum data necessary to provide and improve our services.
03

How We Use Your Data

We use your personal information for the following purposes:

  • Providing, maintaining, and improving the CollabSpace platform
  • Processing transactions and sending related receipts
  • Sending important service notifications and product updates
  • Detecting and preventing fraud or unauthorized access
  • Personalizing your workspace experience
  • Complying with legal obligations
04

Cookies

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your browsing experience and analyze traffic. You can control cookie preferences through your browser settings or our cookie consent manager. We use:

  • Essential Cookies: Required for the platform to function correctly
  • Analytics Cookies: Help us understand how you use the platform (e.g., Google Analytics)
  • Preference Cookies: Remember your settings and preferences
05

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We may share limited data with trusted third parties only in these circumstances:

  • Service providers who help operate our platform (under strict DPAs)
  • Legal authorities when required by law or court order
  • Business transferees in case of a merger or acquisition (with notice)
06

Data Security

We employ industry-standard security measures to protect your data, including:

  • AES-256 encryption for data at rest
  • TLS 1.3 encryption for data in transit
  • Regular third-party security audits and penetration testing
  • Role-based access controls for internal staff
  • 99.9% uptime SLA backed by redundant infrastructure
07

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Rectification: Correct inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Erasure: Request deletion of your personal data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Portability: Receive your data in a machine-readable format
  • Objection: Object to certain types of processing
✅ To exercise any of these rights, email our Data Protection Officer at the address below.
08

Data Retention

We retain your personal data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services. After account deletion, data is securely purged within 90 days, except where retention is required by law.
