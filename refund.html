Refund Policy

We stand behind Tamperline.us with a 14-day money-back guarantee on all plans — Tap, Barrel, and Cellar. If Tamperline.us isn't the right fit for your brewery or retail business within the first 14 days, we'll refund your payment in full, no questions asked.

📅 14 Days Money-back window
💳 Same Method Returned to original payment
⚡ 5–10 Days Bank processing time
01

Overview

We know switching to a new inventory and booking platform is a big decision for your brewery or retail shop. That's why we offer a full 14-day refund on your first subscription charge — no lengthy forms, no pressure. If Tamperline.us doesn't work for your operation, our team will do everything possible to help first, and process your refund quickly if needed.

02

Eligibility

You are eligible for a full refund if you meet all of the following criteria:

  • The refund request is submitted within 14 calendar days of your initial subscription purchase
  • The request is for your first charge on the plan (Tap, Barrel, or Cellar) — not a renewal
  • Your account has not violated our Terms and Conditions (e.g., data scraping, unauthorized use)
  • The subscription was purchased directly through Tamperline.us — not through a third-party reseller
⚠️ Monthly and annual plan renewals are not eligible for refunds. Only the initial purchase qualifies under the 14-day window. Prorated credits may be available for annual plans — see Section 4.
03

Proof of Purchase

To process your refund, you'll need to provide:

  • Your Tamperline.us account email address and registered brewery or business name
  • The transaction ID or receipt number from your subscription confirmation email
  • The plan name you subscribed to (Tap, Barrel, or Cellar)
  • A brief reason for the refund — optional, but genuinely helps us improve for other breweries
04

Prorated Refunds

For annual subscriptions cancelled after the 14-day window, we may issue a prorated account credit at our discretion, based on the unused months remaining on your plan. This credit can be applied to a future Tamperline.us subscription or plan downgrade.

ℹ️ Prorated refunds to your original payment method are not available after the 14-day window has closed. Account credits are issued on a case-by-case basis — contact our support team to discuss your situation.
05

How to Request a Refund

Requesting a refund takes less than 2 minutes:

  1. Log in to your Tamperline.us account and go to Settings → Billing
  2. Click "Request Refund" under your active plan
  3. Fill in your transaction ID and optional reason, then submit
  4. Our support team will respond within 24 hours with confirmation

Alternatively, contact our support team directly using the details in Section 8 below.

06

Processing Timeline

Once your refund request is approved, here's what to expect:

  • Approval confirmation emailed to you within 24–48 hours of your request
  • Refund initiated to your original payment method within 2 business days of approval
  • Funds visible in your bank or card account within 5–10 business days depending on your financial institution
07

Exceptions — When Refunds Are Not Available

Refunds will not be issued in the following situations:

  • The 14-day eligibility window from your initial purchase has passed
  • Your account was suspended or terminated due to a Terms of Service violation
  • The subscription was purchased as part of a promotional bundle or discounted partnership deal
  • Add-on purchases such as additional user seats, integrations, or one-time data migration services
  • Subscription renewals (monthly or annual) — renewals are non-refundable
08

Contact Our Support Team

Can't find your answer here? Our brewery support team is available Monday–Friday, 9am–6pm Mountain Time. We're real people who understand small business operations.
